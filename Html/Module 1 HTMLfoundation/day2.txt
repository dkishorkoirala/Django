Lesson 4: Lists in HTML
Tags:

<ul> - unordered list (bullets)
<ol> - ordered list (numbers)
<li> = list them

ex.
<h3>My Goals</h3>
<ol>
    <li>Learn HTML</li>
    <li>master Django</li>
    <li>Build Projects</li>
</ol>

<h3>Favorite Foods</h3>
<ul> 
    <li>Pizza</li>
    <li>Biryani</li>
    <li>Chocolate</li>
</ul>
